Avocado Vinaigrette

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons - white wine vinegar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons - fresh lemon juice
  • 1 avocado, cubed
  • 1/4 cup - extra virgin olive oil
  • salt to taste
  • pepper to taste

Preparation

  1. Put the vinegar, lemon juice, and the avocado cubes into a blender. Blend until smooth. If it's too thick, add just enough water to get a smooth consistency.
  2. With the blender running, slowly pour in the oil. Add the salt and pepper to taste.
